Center for the Education of Women+

The CEW+ offers workshops, counseling, and financial resources and opportunities for students across the University of Michigan. While the center was founded on the principle to create equitable and inclusive environments for women exploring their potential in higher education, the have expanded to include anyone who is navigating the University of Michigan who are in need of additional support. For more information, please check out the CEW+ website.
